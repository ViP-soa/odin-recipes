You will need:

  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 Chopped onion
  • 1 Ground garlic clove
  • 1 Tablespoon brownsugar
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 Clove
  • 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt

Method of preparation

  1. In a pan, heat the oil and saute the garlic and the onion
  2. Mix the sugar, cook it in low temperature until it has a caramelized texture
  3. Mix the other ingredients and cook it in low temperature for about 10 minutes.
